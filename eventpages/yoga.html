FAQs

What is Face Yoga?
Face Yoga is a natural way to tone and relax facial muscles, improving skin elasticity and promoting a youthful appearance.
When and where will the Face Yoga session take place?
The Face Yoga session will take place on October 25th from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM at ISTE PCCOE.
How can I register for the Face Yoga session?
You can register by clicking the Register button above or visiting our official event page.
What should I bring to the session?
We recommend bringing a small mirror, a towel, and a bottle of water for hydration.
Is Face Yoga suitable for everyone?
Yes, Face Yoga is suitable for all age groups and does not require any prior experience.
